About Rare Gem Media

Built on strategy.
Rooted in craft.

Every brand has a gem inside. We help you find it, polish it, and show it to the world.

After 20 years building digital presence for businesses and organizations, I started Rare Gem Media to be able to give back more and work with clients I truly believe in. I take on a limited number of projects — I'm working full-time and I'm a mom — so every client matters. I only say yes to work that excites me and aligns with what I believe in.

I spent two decades learning how to move the needle — not with shortcuts or templates, but with real strategy, thoughtful design, and work that means something. Along the way, I learned that the best results come when three things align: a clear goal, a deep understanding of who you're talking to, and the creative skill to make something that actually stands out.

Most businesses get stuck in the middle. They hire agencies that treat them like a line item. They buy templates that look like everyone else. They get lost in tactics without strategy. And then they wonder why nothing moves. That's what Rare Gem Media exists to change.

I started this agency because I wanted to work with people who refuse to blend in — businesses that know their brand matters, leaders who understand that investment in real design and strategy actually pays off, and founders who want to build something worth remembering instead of something that checks a box.

What I believe in:

Strategy first. Every design decision is rooted in your goals and your audience. I start with "why," not with trends.
Custom always. No templates. No shortcuts. Every project is built from scratch to fit exactly who you are and what you need.
Story-driven. Your brand has something worth saying. Design is just the vehicle for telling that story beautifully and consistently.
Community matters. I'm rooted in Little Rock, and I invest back through our pro bono program. Real design work should help local businesses grow.
Quality over volume. I take on projects I can do right, not as many as possible. Your success is my success.

If you're looking for a partner who gets it — who understands that good design is an investment, who'll challenge your thinking, and who actually cares about your results — let's talk.

Ready to start a conversation?

Tell me about your project. No obligation, no pressure — just a real conversation about what you're building and how we might help.
