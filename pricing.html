Common Questions

Before you
reach out.

Still have questions? Send a note and we'll get back to you within one business day.

How does payment work?

Projects require a 50% deposit to begin, with the remaining balance due upon final delivery. Retainers are billed monthly. We accept ACH, credit card, and check.

How long does a typical project take?

Brand Essentials typically takes 2–3 weeks. Brand + Web Launch runs 4–6 weeks. Full Digital Presence packages are scoped over 8–10 weeks. Timelines depend on client feedback turnaround.

What's included in revisions?

All packages include one full round of revisions after the initial delivery and launch. Additional revision rounds can be added at an hourly rate of $95/hour.

Can I customize a package?

Absolutely. Packages are a starting point — we can add or swap services to fit your specific needs. Reach out and we'll build a custom scope together.

Do you work with businesses outside Little Rock?

Yes. While we're Little Rock-based, we work with clients remotely across the country. All project communication can be done via email, video call, or your preferred platform.
